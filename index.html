News

  • New work with Pin-Yu Chen (IBM Research): Vision Transformers are Robust Learners.
  • New work with Siddha Ganju (NVIDIA): Flood Segmentation on Sentinel-1 SAR Imagery with Semi-Supervised Learning. This work is based on our winning solution (first runners-up) submitted to this competition organized by the NASA Impact team. We also made it to four NeurIPS 2021 workshops: AI for Science: Mind the Gaps, Tackling Climate Change with Machine Learning, Women in ML, and Machine Learning and the Physical Sciences
  • Masked image modeling with Autoencoders (joint work with Aritra Roy Gosthipaty)
  • New project: Point cloud segmentation in the wild. We provide an implementation of PointNet in TensorFlow for segmenting 3D point clouds along with pre-trained models. Our training scripts are fully compatible with TPUs allowing you to experiment faster. This is a joint work with Soumik Rakshit.
  • New post from the GCP blog: CI/CD for Machine Learning - Part I, Part II (joint work with Chansung Park).
